Our Story

About MIDC Store

Founded in the heart of Maharashtra's industrial belt, MIDC Store was established to bridge the gap between industrial buyers and quality suppliers. We serve factories, SMEs, contractors, and procurement teams with a curated catalog of 12,000+ industrial products.

Our deep roots in the MIDC (Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation) zones give us unparalleled access to competitive pricing, bulk deals, and rapid fulfillment across the state.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower industries with reliable, affordable, and quality industrial products — delivered fast, everywhere.

🔭 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted industrial e-commerce platform by 2028, serving 100,000+ businesses.

✅ ISO Certified

All products meet Indian and international quality standards. 100% genuine goods guaranteed.

🚚 Fast Delivery

Same-day dispatch for in-stock items. Pan-Maharashtra coverage. Bulk orders welcome.
